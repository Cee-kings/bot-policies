Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This policy covers Unscramble Bot and Flag Quiz Bot — two Discord game bots built and maintained by the same developer. It explains exactly what data each bot collects, what it never touches, and how to get your data removed.

What we collect

  • Your Discord username
  • Your Discord user ID
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Custom words submitted through supported bot features, where applicable

That's the full list. It's collected only to make gameplay features — like leaderboards — work.

What we don't collect

Both bots use Discord's Message Content permission to read player answers during a game round. Beyond that single purpose:

  • Message content is never stored or logged after a guess is checked
  • Attachments, images, videos, and links are never collected
  • Conversations in servers where the bots aren't installed are never accessed
  • Message content is never used for advertising or profiling

How we use it

  • Check answers during games
  • Track scores and leaderboards
  • Maintain player statistics across sessions
  • Store custom words, where that feature is used
  • Keep gameplay running smoothly and fix bugs

Where it's stored

Player data is stored in a database hosted by Neon, used only to support the gameplay features above.

Sharing

We don't sell, rent, or share your information for advertising or marketing. Full stop.

Keeping & deleting your data

Your data is kept for as long as it's needed for gameplay. Want it gone? Just ask — see contact details below — and we'll delete it.

Children's privacy

Both bots are intended for use by people who meet Discord's own minimum age requirements. We don't knowingly collect data from anyone below that age.

Changes to this policy

This page may be updated occasionally. Continuing to use either bot after a change means you accept the update.

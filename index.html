Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to Unscramble Bot and Flag Quiz Bot (collectively, the "Bots"), Discord applications developed and maintained independently by the same developer.

Information We Collect

  • Discord username
  • Discord user ID
  • Game scores and statistics
  • Custom words or game content submitted through supported Bot features (where applicable)

This information is collected solely to provide gameplay features.

Information We Do Not Collect

The Bots require the Message Content Intent to validate game answers.

  • Message content is not stored or logged after validation.
  • Attachments, images, videos, and links are not collected.
  • Private conversations outside servers where the Bots are installed are not accessed.
  • Message content is never used for advertising or profiling.

How We Use Information

  • Validate answers during games.
  • Track scores and leaderboards.
  • Maintain player statistics.
  • Store custom words where applicable.
  • Operate and improve gameplay.

Data Storage

Player information is stored in a database hosted by Neon solely for gameplay functionality.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Retention

Gameplay information is retained while needed for gameplay features. Users may request deletion of their stored data.

Children's Privacy

The Bots are intended for Discord users meeting Discord's minimum age requirements.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.
